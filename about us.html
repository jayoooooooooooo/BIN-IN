About Us

BIN:IN – More Than a Business, A Movement for a Greener Future

BIN:IN is a Philippine-based environmental system built to inspire a cleaner, greener future for all. More than a platform, BIN:IN is a movement that empowers communities to take active roles in proper waste disposal and environmental responsibility. Through features like waste tracking, GPS-based bin location tagging, real-time bin condition reporting, and a point-based reward system, BIN:IN turns everyday habits into meaningful change. It’s designed to make waste management simple, accessible, and rewarding. But BIN:IN doesn’t stop there. Users can also shop for eco-friendly merchandise through the platform, with every purchase directly helping fund the installation of trash bins in underserved areas across the country. It’s a system that links personal responsibility with collective progress. At BIN:IN, we believe sustainability isn’t just a goal—it’s a lifestyle. Together, we can turn small, conscious actions into nationwide impact.

Local Partnerships & Sponsorships:

BIN:IN will collaborate with:

•LGUs (Local Government Units) to integrate waste management efforts

•Eco-stores and green businesses for redeemable items and promotions

•Schools and Barangays for reward campaigns and cleanup challenges

•Sustainability Planning (B2B Feature):

•Aggregated data on waste tagging and bin condition reports can be offered to LGUs and environmental NGOs for a fee, to help with planning waste collection routes and community education programs.
